About

Origami SMTP was created when its author could not find a fake SMTP client with TLS support. Later he came to find out that there may have been other options out there he just did not find them in time. So, long story short, he started Origami SMTP. Development is still an ongoing process and the author hopes to add fake authentication and better support to the servers generation of MHTML files.

Origami SMTP is a fake server that supports TLS and the very basic SMTP commands. It can also validate successfully if the Origami SMTP Certificate Authority is installed.
